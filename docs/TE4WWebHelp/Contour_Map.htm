Contour Map

The Contour Map tool creates a topographic map on the entire terrain that portrays differences in terrain elevation by coloring terrain according to varying altitudes and by connecting points of equal elevation with contour lines. The contour colors and lines are rendered on the terrain as well as on 3DML layers.

To display a contour map:

§  On the sidebar, click the Analysis tab > switch on the Contour Map. A contour map overlays the entire 3D Window, and the contour map’s check mark is highlighted in the sidebar.

To set the contour map mode:

§  On the sidebar, click Settings  and then in the sidebar’s Analysis section, under Contour Map select one of the following:

§  Colors - Apply different colors from a contour palette to each elevation level.

§  Colors and Lines - Apply different colors from a contour palette to each elevation level and connect points of equal elevation with contour lines.

Note:        The contour map will only be displayed if it was switched on from the Analysis tab.
