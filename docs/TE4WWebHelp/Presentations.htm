Presentations

To play a presentation or view list of presentation steps:

1.     On the sidebar, click the Navigate tab, and then in the Locations and Presentations section, click the presentation to play.

2.     To play the presentation, click Play . The following additional presentation commands are available:

§  Jump to Previous

§  Jump to Next

§  Pause

§  Open Presentation Steps .

3.     To open a list of presentation steps, click Presentation Steps .
