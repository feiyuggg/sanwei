Distance and Slope Tool

To measure aerial/horizontal distance, elevation difference, slope, and ground distance:

1.     On the sidebar, click the Analysis tab > Distance.

2.     Click any point on the terrain to define the start point of the measurement. Click to set the second point and then again for each additional segment. Right-click or click the check box  to finish the measurement. Before right-clicking, you can click Delete Point  to delete the last point. The distance polyline is colored in yellow and the distance measurement is saved and available from the Objects section of the sidebar’s Analysis tab. The Distance Result dialog box displays total aerial distance, total horizontal distance, elevation difference, and slope.

3.     Click Ground Distance  to calculate the distance on the terrain surface. The ground distance polyline is colored in white, and total ground distance is added to the measurements in the Distance Result dialog box.

4.     If you want to delete the measurement polyline, click Delete .
