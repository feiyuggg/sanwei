Saving and Displaying Measurement, Volume and Analysis Objects

All distance, area, and volume measurements and viewsheds can be saved in the project and their display switched on/off from the Analysis tab.

To save a measurement or analysis object:

§  On the sidebar, click Settings  and then in the sidebar’s Analysis section, under Analysis Objects, select one of the following options:

§  Save on this computer – Create a measurement object and save it on your computer, so that it will be available if the TE4W page is closed and then reopened.

§  Save until exit page – Create a measurement object and save it only as long as the TE4W page is open.

§  Delete when close tool – Delete measurements immediately upon closing their respective tools.

To display a measurement or analysis object:

§  On the sidebar, click Analysis, and then in the Objects section, switch on the required object.
