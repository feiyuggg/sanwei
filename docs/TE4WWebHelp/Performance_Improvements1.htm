Performance Improvements

§  Large feature layers – New mechanism speeds up rendering of even massive point feature layers for a smoother flight experience.

§  Point clouds – Point clouds now load and display in TE4W as fast as in TerraExplorer Desktop, due to modifications to CPT point size and level of detail requests.

§  Models – Improvements to floor mechanism have vastly accelerated the loading of models.
