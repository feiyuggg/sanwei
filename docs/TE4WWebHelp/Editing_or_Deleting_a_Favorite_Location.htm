Editing or Deleting a Favorite Location

To edit or delete a favorite location:

1.     On the sidebar, click the Navigate tab, and then in the Locations and Presentations section, click the location you want to edit.

2.     To modify properties, click Edit . The My Locations dialog opens. Modify the properties as required. See “Adding a Favorite Location” in this chapter for more information.

3.     To delete, click Delete .
